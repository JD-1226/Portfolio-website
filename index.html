About
Me

I’m Pravin, a designer with a background in Computer Science and Cyber Security. My journey began with projects in NLP, deep learning, and safety-focused applications.
Over time, I found my passion for UX/UI design and shifted towards creating user-centered experiences. I combine technical insight with creativity to design solutions that are functional and visually engaging. My approach emphasizes problem-solving, design thinking, and clear visual storytelling. I strive to create platforms with usability, clarity, and enjoyable micro-interactions. From product research and strategy to interface design, I enjoy shaping the entire experience. Through my academic journey and hands-on projects, I’ve developed a variety of skills. I adapt quickly, stay curious, and view every project as an opportunity to grow. My goal is to design experiences that make technology intuitive, impactful, and focused on people.
